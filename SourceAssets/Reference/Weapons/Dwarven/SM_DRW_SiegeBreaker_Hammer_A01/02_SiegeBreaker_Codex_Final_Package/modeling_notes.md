# Siege Breaker Modeling Notes

## Canonical geometry
The hammer is centered on the Z axis. The head is symmetrical across X and primarily symmetrical across Y, while front and back ornamentation may differ.

## Head blockout
- full envelope: 52 × 32 × 38 cm
- central core width: 24 cm
- left stone block width: 14 cm
- right stone block width: 14 cm
- model large chips only where they alter silhouette

## Shaft and overlap
The structural shaft is 118 cm from Z 14 to132. It penetrates the pommel by 4 cm. This resolves the apparent 174 cm sum while preserving the 170 cm overall length.

## Detail hierarchy
- modeled: stone block silhouette, core plates, major braces, collars, pommel facets
- modeled or baked depending camera distance: large runes and deep engravings
- baked: fine cracks, leather grain, small scratches, micro-pitting

## Pivot and export
Place the asset origin at the bottom center of the pommel. Apply transforms. Keep Z up.
