# Light Meks

## Lore Entry

Light Meks are not battlefield engines in the same class as heavy Gnome war
frames. They are gnome-sized powered suits built around a compact Aetherium
Power Core, designed to amplify a Mekgineer's mobility, stability, protection,
and weapon handling without hiding the operator's identity.

A Light Mek should still read as a gnome in a suit. The wearer keeps gnome-like
proportions: compact adult body mass, sturdy legs, practical hands, oversized
work boots or armored foot housings, and controlled expressive ears rather than
huge inflated ear silhouettes. The suit hands must preserve the gnome hand rule:
four fingers plus one thumb on each hand. The operator is not stretched into a
human shape, and the suit is not an oversized vehicle frame.

The core of every Light Mek is a small Aetherium Power Core mounted in the chest,
back, or reinforced torso harness. The core feeds assisted joints, grip
stabilizers, shield emitters, recoil dampers, and limited burst movement. Gnome
Mekgineers value the Light Mek because it lets a small operator carry heavier
field equipment, brace weapon recoil, and survive close-range battlefield chaos
without giving up the speed and cleverness that define gnome combat engineering.

Light Mek pilots usually fight with pistols or a rifle rather than integrated
oversized arm cannons. The suit has three weapon hardpoints: two pistol
hardpoints and one rifle hardpoint. The pistol hardpoints are normally mounted
at the hips, thighs, belt, or compact side rails for fast draw and retention.
The rifle hardpoint is normally mounted along the back, shoulder line, or a
stabilized rear clamp so the operator can carry one compact rifle without
turning the suit into a heavy Mek platform.

Every Light Mek carries a small refractor shield. This shield is not a massive
wall projector. It is a personal defensive lens or emitter array that can flare
briefly to catch incoming force, redirect heat, or bend hostile energy away from
the operator. When the Aetherium Power Core builds up too much unstable charge,
the refractor shield can vent excess aetheric energy in a damaging discharge.
Skilled pilots use this discharge as a close-range deterrent, punishing enemies
that press too close while keeping the suit light enough for skirmish movement.

Among gnomes, Light Meks are favored by scouts, duelists, field engineers,
Mekgineer guards, and mobile rifle crews. They are practical, personal, and
clever rather than grandiose. A Heavy Mek is a walking war machine. A Light Mek
is a gnome-sized suit that lets one brilliant, stubborn operator survive battles
that should be far too large for them.

## Canon Constraints

- Light Meks are gnome-sized powered suits, not heavy Mek vehicles.
- The operator remains visibly gnome-like in proportion and identity.
- Gnome hands remain four fingers plus one thumb on each hand.
- Ears are compact and expressive; do not use huge ear silhouettes.
- A compact Aetherium Power Core is required.
- Three weapon hardpoints are required: two pistol hardpoints and one rifle hardpoint.
- Weapon use is carried or mounted pistols/rifle, not oversized integrated heavy cannons.
- A small refractor shield is required.
- The refractor shield can occasionally discharge excess aetheric energy and damage nearby enemies.
- Visual language should stay Gnome/Mekgineer: brass, copper, dark iron, leather, compact engineering, and restrained blue Aetherium glow.

## Image Approval Criteria

When Flamestrike creates Light Mek concept images for approval, each candidate
should be checked against these criteria before moving into a production package:

1. The body reads as a gnome-sized suit, not a human, Ogre, or heavy Mek frame.
2. The operator proportions remain compact, sturdy, and adult.
3. The ears are present or implied but not oversized.
4. The hands or gauntlets support four fingers plus one thumb.
5. The Aetherium Power Core is visible and central to the design.
6. The two pistol hardpoints and one rifle hardpoint are clear.
7. The refractor shield is visible as a small personal defensive device.
8. The shield-discharge idea is implied through sockets, emitter lenses, or controlled blue energy accents.
9. The suit can plausibly move fast enough for scouts, guards, duelists, or field engineers.
10. The design stays distinct from `SK_GNM_HeavyMek_Rivalry_A01` and `SK_GNM_IonaSiegebreakerMek_A01`.
