# Gameplay Canon

## Project Gameplay Direction

The MMORPG target is a classic-style original fantasy MMORPG with server-authoritative gameplay, data-driven content, readable combat, questing, loot, vendors, trainers, gathering, crafting, dungeons, party/social systems, and long-form world progression.

Server-authoritative ownership covers combat results, movement-critical state, health, death, resurrection, hardcore state, XP, leveling, inventory, equipment, loot, currency, quest acceptance/progress/completion/rewards, vendor transactions, crafting/gathering outputs, mail, auction house, guild/party state, PvP results, and ranking.

## Shared Stats And Resources

Shared stat names include Strength, Stamina, Intelligence, Wisdom, Dexterity, Critical Strike, Parry Chance, and Block Chance.

Shared class resources include Hit Points, Mana Points, Fury Points, Tenacity Points, Energy Points, Devotion Points, and class-appropriate variants.

## Item Rarity

Both MMORPG and ARPG planning share a seven-tier launch rarity ladder:

| Tier | Technical Tier | Display Name | Player Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Junk | Broken | Sell or salvage |
| 1 | Common | Mundane | Basic gear |
| 2 | Uncommon | Attuned | First real upgrades |
| 3 | Rare | Relic | Good gear and dungeon-quality rewards |
| 4 | Epic | Ancient | High-end gear |
| 5 | Legendary | Mythic | Iconic chase gear |
| 6 | Artifact | Primordial | World-defining items |

Quest remains a special non-power marker.

## Mekgineer Gameplay

MMORPG Mekgineers begin levels 1-9 in a pre-Mek apprentice phase with an AetherTek pistol and spanner. The first Mek part hunt mixes professions, quests, exploration, and combat loot. The first recovered Mek piece is an old Mek chest plate with a faded maker's mark found in abandoned workshop ruins after defeating a rift-touched mechanical golem.

Gyro is the first Mekgineer mentor, a retired Gnomish Mekgineer with an old Heavy Frame Mek in or near his forest-edge shop. The old workshop ownership remains unresolved.

Mekgineer progression differs by project. MMORPG progression uses levels 1-9 pre-Mek, 10-20 compact armor, 21-40 heavy suit, and 41-60 large frame. ARPG Mekgineers begin with the first Mek at level 1, then receive Mek milestones at levels 20 and 40.

Mekgineer talent trees are Mek Pilot, Armor Systems, and Weapon Systems. Mekgineer resources are Aether Charge and Energy. Energy supports basic operation and quick shots. Aether Charge supports charged blasts and high-impact weapon use.

## ARPG Gameplay Direction

The ARPG derivative is a Diablo II-style fixed-isometric action RPG path that remains tied to the MMORPG lore foundation. It is single-player first, co-op-ready early, with optional 2-3 player co-op later.

The ARPG is darker, faster, more immediate, exploratory, and quest-driven rather than mass-horde loot-driven. Combat is quick and deadly but not easy.

Approved first ARPG classes are Warrior, Ranger, Paladin, Arcane Knight, and Mekgineer. Warrior is first in implementation order. Priest remains a later class lane and MMORPG class candidate.
