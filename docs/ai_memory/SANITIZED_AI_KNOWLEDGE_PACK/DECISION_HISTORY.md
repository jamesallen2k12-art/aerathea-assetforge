# Decision History

## 2026-06-17

Project structure for the MMORPG path was approved. A classic-style original MMORPG remained the long-term target. The visual target became Aetherium Classic+: classic readability with Unreal lighting, stronger atmosphere, cleaner models, better materials, and original IP protection.

## 2026-06-21

A separate ARPG derivative path was created. The working title became `Aerathea: Honor and Glory`. The ARPG became a precursor parallel commercial project and lore foundation for the MMORPG.

The ARPG first camera decision became fixed isometric first, with rotatable camera deferred. Controls became keyboard/mouse first with controller compatibility planned early.

The ARPG tone became darker and more menacing than the MMORPG starter experience, with storm-night opening, fog, lightning, night-to-day progression, fog of war, and light-radius gameplay.

The first ARPG class order became Warrior, Ranger, Paladin, Arcane Knight, and Mekgineer. Paladin replaced Priest in the first ARPG roster because the ARPG is more combat-forward. Priest remains a later class lane.

Shared item rarity names became Broken, Mundane, Attuned, Relic, Ancient, Mythic, and Primordial, with Quest as a special non-power marker.

## 2026-06-22

Gnome anatomy became locked at four fingers plus one thumb. The rule applies to visible hands, gloves, gauntlets, Mek controls, rigs, animations, and concept hands.

The Dark Elf house name became House Nytherion. Nytharion became deprecated source-sheet spelling.

Valar became an active visual source/reference label with final player-facing name approval still pending originality/IP review.

## 2026-06-24 To 2026-06-28

The Aerathea Unreal review scene became the primary controlled production review surface. First-pass DCC/Unreal validation expanded across common props, Mekgineer gear, gnome/ogre rivalry assets, Infernal assets, Abyss/Anathema assets, materials, VFX, and native-backed Blueprint wrappers.

A multi-specialist workflow was established for lead coordination, visual direction, production packages, DCC preparation, Unreal implementation, gameplay systems, VFX/materials, QA/validation, and docs/index maintenance.

## 2026-06-28

The Infernal starter lane gained validated first-pass implementations for Mage, Warrior, Rogue, Hunter, horn-wing arch, AbyssalSpellcasting VFX, BrandGlow states, WorthinessJudgment VFX, worthiness altar, and ritual altar Blueprint behavior.

The 10 m universal portal rebuild completed as a validated first-pass scale/composition implementation. Final art, VFX, audio, and traversal signoff remain later work.

The Giant base scale was regenerated and validated to current A04 baselines: female 442 cm and male 470 cm.
