## Culture Anchors

Gnomes are compact makers, inventors, Mekgineers, scouts, and gadgeteers. Dwarves are broad, dense, mountain-forged crafters and fortress builders. Elves are graceful, ancient, nature-bound, moonlit, musical, and artisan-focused. Dark Elves are elegant, shadowed, moonlit, secretive, oath-bound, refined, dangerous, and ancient.

Orcs are noble, upright, straight-backed, spiritual, clan-loyal, disciplined, and powerful. Minotaurs are plains and lowlands people, brutal, ruthless, strength-respecting, darkness-born, and magic-resistant. Ogres are oversized war-created people with crude Teknomancy and cairn/forge settlement language.

Drakhar are small desert lizardfolk with a magical obsession and Volcreon-linked motives. Giants include civilized stoneworkers and highland nomads, with Blood Axe Tribe language separated from neutral Giant culture. Valar are human-like highborn northern warrior-priests and oathkeepers. Anubisath/Sutekh and Basari have approved scale anchors with deeper culture still pending dedicated intake.
