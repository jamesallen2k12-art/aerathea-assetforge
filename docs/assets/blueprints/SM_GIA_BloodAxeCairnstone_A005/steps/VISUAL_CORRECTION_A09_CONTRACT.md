## Goal

Correct the visually rejected A08 base by rebuilding the masonry and rubble as
independently reviewable, exactly reattachable modules whose assembled render
has no white geometry leaks, preserves source detail, and reads as irregular
ovoid masonry rather than a circular segmented ring.

## Required Construction

1. Preserve exact A04 C001 geometry and UV signatures.
2. Build C002, C003, and C004 as independent mesh modules at the shared asset
   origin; do not destructively split or reuse A08 course geometry.
3. Add named shared-frame anchors for C001, C002, C003, C004, and assembled
   root. Anchor transforms must be deterministic and audit-exact.
4. Retain maximum course extents and stone-count authority while replacing
   the smooth radial wedge formula with source-conditioned irregular ovoid
   stone contours.
5. Use hidden inset receiver/mortar geometry to cover every visible interface;
   literal air must never resolve to the review background from an approved
   camera.
6. Rebuild C004 as staggered clustered rubble with varied radial depth and
   crown height, not one evenly spaced annular necklace.
7. Preserve source-owned color without grading and correct exposed-face UV
   routing so detail is not vertically smeared or replaced by flat fill.
8. Export each base module separately and also export the assembled LOD set.

- No disconnected-rubble failure in the final proof.
