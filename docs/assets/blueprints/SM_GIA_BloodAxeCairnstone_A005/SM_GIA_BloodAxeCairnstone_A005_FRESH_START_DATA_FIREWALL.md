# SM_GIA_BloodAxeCairnstone_A005 Fresh-Start Data Firewall

Status: approved Step 01 fresh-start firewall

Artifact classification: `authoritative`

Date: 2026-07-15

## Purpose

Prevent any legacy Cairn Stone asset-specific data from entering the A005
fresh-start project while preserving legacy artifacts as untouched history.

## Allowed Asset-Specific Inputs

Only after the step that governs their use:

- the confirmed A02 PNG at its existing tracked path;
- its exact scanline target, scan record, rebuild, difference image, and
  manifest;
- exact integer coordinates derived in approved A005 steps;
- source-authored dimension labels and endpoints;
- fresh formulas, manifests, and explicit Flamestrike decisions produced by
  approved A005 steps.

Step 01 records the source and manifest paths as reference strings only. It
does not inspect or promote them.

## Allowed General Process Inputs

- Core and the approved fresh-start plan;
- generic AetherForge measurement and validation rules;
- the formal game-ready pipeline;
- generic naming, LOD, collision, UV, material, export, Unreal, and performance
  rules;
- generic validators containing no Cairn Stone constants or generated assets.

## Blocked Asset-Specific Inputs

- all A001-A004 generated meshes and DCC sources;
- all A001-A004 crops, masks, formulas, centers, measurements, component IDs,
  anchors, and source-derived constants;
- all A001-A004 textures, UVs, normals, materials, LODs, collision, exports,
  Unreal assets, and review renders;
- old Cairn Stone builders or generated scripts as implementation templates;
- old manifests as source authority;
- old proof boards as visual-fitting references;
- old procedural primitives, analytic footprints, or inferred envelopes;
- remembered component counts or dimensions not re-derived from A02;
- external web references, concept variants, or other Cairn Stone images;
- legacy output relabeled as A005 work.

Legacy records must not be opened by an A005 step unless a new Core block leads
to a specifically approved historical-audit contract.

## Script Isolation

Future A005 asset scripts must use the A005 namespace, declare all
asset-specific constants in fresh manifests, read no A001-A004 output path,
fail closed on missing authority, and support non-mutating validation before
opening output paths.

Step 01 authorizes no asset script. The project recovery checkpoint utility is
the only script used under the approved checkpoint protocol.

## Evidence And Interpretation Boundary

No interpretation may enter an exact-measurement manifest. A005 measurement
reviews may not show candidate fills, guessed hidden surfaces, smoothed
envelopes, or geometry previews. Interpretation requires its own later named
approval gate.

## Enforcement

If blocked data is accessed, copied, referenced as authority, or found in an
A005 output, stop production, preserve the affected artifact, classify it, and
enter Core Recovery. Do not repair forward.

## Step 01 Access Declaration

- No A001-A004 asset-specific artifact was opened or read.
- No A02 source pixel or scanline file was opened or read.
- No source or production asset was created, modified, copied, or deleted.
- A005 contains governance records only.

## Approval Gate

Flamestrike approved this firewall on 2026-07-15. Approval does not authorize
source inspection or Step 02.
