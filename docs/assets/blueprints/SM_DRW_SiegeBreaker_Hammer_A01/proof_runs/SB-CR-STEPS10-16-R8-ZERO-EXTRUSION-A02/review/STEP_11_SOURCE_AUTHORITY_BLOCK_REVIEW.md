## What Is Exact And Valid

- All six whole-hammer source scans and their exact pixels.
- Overall length, front width, the two final candidate depths, and front-owned
  longitudinal stations.
- Component names and qualitative source-view roles.
- The approved equations for rotational surfaces, haft wrapping, hidden ruled
  closure, and one Rz180 completion.

## What Is Missing

  right stone, positive-X strike face, and upper coupler.
- Exact source pixels or boundary loops defining the negative spaces that must
  remain empty.
- Exact corresponding front/right/top/bottom boundary edges required to build
  the approved ruled joins.

Whole-hammer scanlines cannot answer those component-owner questions by
themselves. The old meshes and old component intervals are forbidden.

## Decision
