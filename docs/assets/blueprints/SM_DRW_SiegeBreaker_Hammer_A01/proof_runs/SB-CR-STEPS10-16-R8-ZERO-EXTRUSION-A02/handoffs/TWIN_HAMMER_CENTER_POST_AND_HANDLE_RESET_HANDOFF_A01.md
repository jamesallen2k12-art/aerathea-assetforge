## Plain-English Resume Summary

The latest pair is aligned, mirrored, welded, manifold, and dimensionally
identical, but it is the wrong shape.

The rebuild used the front drawing like a cookie cutter:

- the head was carried straight through the full depth, filling the visible
  separation around the center post; and
- the handle was closed between flat front, back, and side planes, making it
  square.

Both errors exist in both hammers because the pair has the same saved geometry
hash. They are not camera, material, or shading errors.
