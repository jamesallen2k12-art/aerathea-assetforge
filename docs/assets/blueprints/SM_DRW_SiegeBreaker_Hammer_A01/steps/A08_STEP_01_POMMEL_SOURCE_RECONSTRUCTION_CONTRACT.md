## Exact Scope

1. Verify the source hash and numeric pommel interval before Blender starts.
2. Construct a fresh isolated pommel from no predecessor mesh.
3. Keep the pommel inside `Z=0..18 cm` with maximum width `11 cm` and pivot at
   bottom-center `[0,0,0]`.
4. Reproduce the visible faceted body, upper/lower collars, layered front
   diamond plate, metal bracing, and restrained blue focal rune.
5. Treat depth, rear continuation, internal attachment, and unseen faces as
   `candidate interpretation`, not authority.
6. Create one `.blend`, one validation manifest, and one Blender-rendered
   source-versus-candidate review image.
7. Open the exact review image visibly after a passing technical audit.

- Any A01-A07 Siege Breaker mesh or texture as an input.
- Grip, shaft, head, full-asset assembly, UV texture package, LOD, collision,
  FBX/GLB export, Unreal, or `Fully game-ready` work.
