## Locked Numeric Invariants

- Overall bounds: `52 x 32 x 170 cm`.
- Head: `X +/-26`, `Y +/-16`, `Z 132-170 cm`.
- Shaft: `Z 14-132 cm`, `5 cm` diameter.
- Grip: `Z 18-60 cm`, `42 cm` visible length, no envelope beyond `5 cm` diameter.
- Pommel: `Z 0-18 cm`, `11 cm` maximum width.
- Shaft/pommel overlap: `4 cm` from `Z 14-18 cm`.
- Pivot: bottom-center of pommel at world origin.

## Required Fidelity Corrections

- Dark, massive, irregular faceted runestone head masses rather than pale
  beveled slabs.
- Layered steel and aged-bronze dwarven bracing with a deep central mechanism.
- Recessed, restrained blue rune channels and focal gems.
- Readable dark-brown crisscross leather grip geometry.
- Engraved or inlaid shaft panels with controlled metal bands.
- Faceted, layered counterweight pommel with a rune-crystal focal inset.
- Strong material separation and production-readable wear at review distance.

## Allowed Outputs

- Versioned A02 Blender source, texture package, LOD0-LOD3, collision, FBX/GLB,
  manifests, render scripts, audit scripts, orthographic proof, and one final

- Scoped status and recovery records.

## Blocked Outputs

- Unreal import or Step 17+.
- Changes to locked numeric authority, visual canon, or unrelated worktree files.
- Reclassification as `Fully game-ready` or approved-library content.
- Destructive overwrite or deletion of the A01 candidate.

## Pass Gate

The A02 candidate must pass the numeric, UV, material, LOD, collision, export,
and clean-reimport gates; include all six required canon-defining feature
groups; and produce one non-black visible review image. Final aesthetic
acceptance remains Flamestrike's decision.
