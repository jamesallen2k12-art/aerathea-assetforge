## One Decision

Can the verified final package produce one source-faithful, technically valid
`DCC game-ready candidate` without legacy coordinate contamination, silent
dimension changes, concept-panel geometry overrides, or Unreal escalation?

## Governing Core Principle

First Principles fixes the goal and authority; Stop-the-line rejects source
conflicts; Kaizen selects the smallest complete implementation; time remains a
primary production resource.

- project `AGENTS.md`;
- the verified final ZIP and extracted immutable contents;
- embedded `asset_spec.json`, `dimensions_cm.csv`, component and material specs;

## Allowed Outputs

- fresh Siege Breaker charter, source lock, formulas, measurement contracts,
  ownership records, construction blueprint, validation and handoff records;
- corrected canonical blockout and deterministic orthographic evidence;
- fresh detailed Blender candidate, authored UV/PBR maps and materials;
- LOD0-LOD3, collision, FBX/GLB package, manifests, final DCC renders;
- checkpoints, scoped technical closeout, and visible final review.

## Blocked Outputs

- reuse of the obsolete 2026-07-07 builder or coordinate layout;
- geometry derived from independently illustrated concept panels;
- hidden averaging or silent envelope changes;
- Unreal import, Step 17+, `Fully game-ready`, approved-library promotion, or
  self-approval of final visual quality;
- modification or staging of unrelated dirty-worktree files.

## Validators

- ZIP integrity and SHA-256;
- lossless RGB scanline proof for the style reference;
- exact numeric bounds and interval checks;
- component, contact, material-slot, UV, triangle, LOD, collision, export, and
  clean imported-FBX audits;
- deterministic render-manifest consistency;
- final image inspection before visible presentation.

## Status Rules

- Pass: objective Step 16 result is `authoritative` as technical evidence and
  the model is a `candidate` / `DCC game-ready candidate`.
- Failure: affected output is `invalid` or `quarantined`; return to the last
  passing numeric authority and do not repair forward.
- Final aesthetic status always remains pending Flamestrike.
