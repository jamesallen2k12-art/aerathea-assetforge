## Drift Cause

The method assumed that exact envelope dimensions, required component
presence, real 360-degree volume, and a technical audit could serve as the
foundation of Hero fidelity. It failed to reproduce the concept's sculptural
forms, layered hard-surface design, authored asymmetry, materials, and
high-frequency visual identity. Technical completeness was allowed to stand in
for visual fidelity.
