## Drift Cause

The active authority chain classified the concept sheet as `style only` and did
not permit its pixels to control geometry. A01/A02 therefore satisfied the
numeric envelope and technical feature checklist without reproducing the
source-view silhouette, component boundaries, proportions, landmark placement,
or rendered material/color distribution.

The generated A02 result visibly substitutes pale extruded slabs for craggy
dark runestone masses, a flat X-frame for layered architectural bracing,
isolated emissive diamonds for continuous engraved metalwork, surface lacing
for a densely wrapped leather grip, and an elongated rounded pommel for the
compact faceted source form.
