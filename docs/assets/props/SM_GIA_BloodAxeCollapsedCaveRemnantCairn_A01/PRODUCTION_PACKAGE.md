`SM_GIA_BloodAxeCollapsedCaveRemnantCairn_A01` is a static abandoned Blood Axe Giant cave-remnant prop. It should read as a once-placed low cairn that has slumped out of order over time: a few oversized stones have shifted, one cap mass has rolled or settled low, ash and mud have gathered into the contact seams, and any red cloth or paint is faded and restrained.

## LOD Plan

All future static mesh implementation requires LOD0, LOD1, LOD2, and LOD3 before shipping use.

- LOD0: full low collapsed cairn silhouette, all major shifted stones, broad ash/mud contact, optional low cloth or paint, and broad rope/rawhide if present.
- LOD1: reduce stone edge loops, remove small chip geometry, simplify cloth edge cuts, reduce underside detail, and simplify rope lashings.
- LOD2: preserve main slumped cairn mass, largest stone planes, broad ground contact, and one restrained Blood Axe color block if present.
- LOD3: simple low collapsed silhouette preserving the cave-remnant read only.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny cracks, soot speckles, lichen, cloth fibers, rope fibers, ash flecks, paint chips, and small stone pitting.
2. Secondary cloth fray, minor stone edge chips, small ground grit, tiny knots, and hidden underside cuts.
3. Optional small debris, horn/bone/iron accents, secondary rope lashings, and duplicate base details.
4. Backside detail, secondary decorative cuts, and extra ash/mud undulation.
5. Stone bevel density, cloth fold geometry, and large base subdivisions.
6. Major shifted-stone silhouette, low collapsed height, and cave-edge ground contact only after all smaller detail is removed.

Never preserve micro-detail at the expense of the collapsed cave-remnant silhouette, Giant-scale read, or Blood Axe material restraint.
