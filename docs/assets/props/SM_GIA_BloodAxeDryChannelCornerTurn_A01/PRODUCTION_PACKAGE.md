`SM_GIA_BloodAxeDryChannelCornerTurn_A01` is a low, heavy, L-shaped dry channel stone for abandoned Blood Axe ritual-stone layouts. It provides static corner variety inside the dry channel set without implying liquid flow, spline behavior, directional conduit logic, aura markings, navigation support, quest guidance, damage lanes, or any runtime function.

The asset should read as a broken Giant-scale stone corner: broad eroded trough lips, dark ash settled into the angled recess, soot-stained slab faces, chipped outer corners, and optional large blackened iron repair clamps used sparingly. The turn is a visual remnant only. It should feel dry, inactive, abandoned, and brutal, not active, magical, wet, readable, or mechanically useful.

Blood Axe identity must remain separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture. This prop may use rough highland stone, soot, ash, trampled mud, blackened iron, rawhide, rope, dull bone, sparse horn, and controlled oxide red accents. It must not use refined cave-town masonry, blue-gray civic stonework, terrace or waterwork craft language, warm hearth settlement cues, peaceful guide symbols, restrained blue runes, or polished civilized Giant finish.
