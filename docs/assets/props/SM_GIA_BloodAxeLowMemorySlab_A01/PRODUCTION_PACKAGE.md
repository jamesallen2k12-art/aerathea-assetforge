`SM_GIA_BloodAxeLowMemorySlab_A01` is a squat abandoned slab left where a Blood Axe nomad camp has moved on. It should read as a cold memory marker: soot on the top plane, ash caught around the base, chipped edges, one old red cloth tie, and rough highland stone worn by weather and camp use.

This is not a functional altar, offering station, loot container, pickup, ritual device, interaction prop, damage source, aura marker, VFX/audio hook, or readable text surface. The design must stay as static environmental history only.

Blood Axe visual language remains limited to a hostile Giant sub-faction. The slab must not redefine neutral/civilized Giant culture, which remains tied to hidden highland settlements, master stonework, civic blue-gray masonry, terraces, waterworks, warm hearth identity, and restrained blue rune language.

## LOD Plan

All future static mesh implementation requires LOD0-LOD3 before shipping use.

- LOD0: full low slab silhouette, broad chipped corners, uneven underside, soot/ash material zones, fixed old red cloth tie, and authored material detail.
- LOD1: 55-65 percent of LOD0; reduce minor bevels, underside cuts, small chip shapes, cloth fold subdivisions, and hidden backside detail.
- LOD2: 30-40 percent of LOD0; preserve the slab footprint, broad top value, chipped outline, and red cloth read while flattening secondary cuts.
- LOD3: 10-18 percent of LOD0; preserve the low stone mass and one broad red cloth or soot/ash value beat only.

Reduction order:

1. Fine cracks, soot speckles, ash flecks, lichen flecks, cloth weave, tiny frays, pigment scratches, and hairline stone grain.
2. Minor bevels, small chips, underside notches, secondary cloth folds, and small mud-contact cuts.
3. Hidden underside detail, backside break planes, and low-value ground-contact shapes.
4. Small cloth knot cuts, small lashing hints, and non-silhouette accents.
5. Secondary slab plane cuts and non-primary chipped edges.
6. Only after small detail is removed, simplify the primary low slab silhouette.

Never reduce the Giant-scale low slab read, moved-camp memory identity, Blood Axe red cloth beat, or soot/ash grounding before removing small detail.
