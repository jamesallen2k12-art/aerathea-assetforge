`SM_GIA_BloodAxeGroundBedding_A01` is a low, ground-level sleeping spread for hostile Blood Axe Giant camps. It should read as a crude hide mat pressed into trampled dirt: broad rough hide sheets, a shallow body depression, dark soot and mud staining, a sparse dirty fur edge, and one restrained oxide red Blood Axe repair strip or tag.

Blood Axe visual language must stay separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture. This prop belongs to a hostile Giant raider sub-faction and should feel temporary, dirty, practical, and harsh. It must not become refined highland bedding, cave-town domestic dressing, civic stoneworker craft, warm hearth blankets, blue-rune Giant culture, or a general neutral Giant sleeping prop.

## 10. LOD Plan

All shipping-quality versions require LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: full low bedding footprint, broad irregular hide edges, central depression, large folds, sparse fur-edge clumps, red cloth accent, and ground-contact deformation.
- LOD1: 55-65 percent of LOD0; reduce minor fold cuts, fur-edge subdivisions, small perimeter waviness, depression rim subdivisions, and red cloth fold cuts.
- LOD2: 25-40 percent of LOD0; preserve the long low hide shape, central darker depression read, sparse fur-edge color block, and red accent while flattening secondary wrinkles.
- LOD3: 10-20 percent of LOD0; preserve a simple oversized ground-mat silhouette with dark hide block, subtle depression color, and one red accent.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny stitches, cloth weave, hide pores, soot speckles, ash flecks, mud flecks, pitting, dirt cracks, and fine scratches.
2. Small fray fibers, fine edge waviness, minor fur cuts, and tiny hide tears.
3. Secondary wrinkles, small compression ridges, and non-silhouette dirt ruts.
4. Red accent fold bevels, optional tie-remnant undercuts, and hidden underside details.
5. Minor depression-rim subdivisions and nonessential perimeter asymmetry.

Never reduce the primary 450-620 cm Giant-scale footprint, low ground-bedding read, central depression, sparse fur-edge read, or Blood Axe red accent before removing small detail.
