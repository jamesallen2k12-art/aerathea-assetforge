Its job is to communicate hostile Giant occupation through environmental storytelling without becoming a functional marker or gameplay object.

## 10. LOD Plan

All future important versions require LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: Full leaning shield rim, broad blackened plate, major bends, large dents, low cairn or stake support, broad lashings, ash/mud base, and readable red paint smear if used.
- LOD1: 60-70 percent of LOD0; simplify plate bends, reduce stone bevels, merge small lashings, reduce underside cuts, and flatten minor ash/mud edges.
- LOD2: 35-45 percent of LOD0; preserve leaning rim silhouette, plate mass, support read, and Blood Axe paint/blackened metal language while baking smaller overlap details into textures.
- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0; preserve only the broad tilted shield scrap, simple support mass, and base footprint; remove small lashings, minor dents, back-side detail, small chips, and most ash geometry.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny scratches, pitting, soot speckles, ash flecks, paint chips, and hairline cracks.
2. Minor rope fibers, small hide wrinkles, small mud ridges, and shallow secondary dents.
3. Small stone bevels, underside cuts, and back-facing support detail.
4. Secondary bends in the plate and non-essential rim cracks.
5. Non-essential base ash shapes and hidden contact cuts.

Never reduce the primary leaning shield-rim silhouette, blackened plate mass, low cairn or stake support read, or hostile Blood Axe red/black material language before removing micro-detail.
