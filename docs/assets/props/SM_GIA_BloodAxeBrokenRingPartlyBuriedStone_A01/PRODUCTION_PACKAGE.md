`SM_GIA_BloodAxeBrokenRingPartlyBuriedStone_A01` defines one low partly buried former ring stone from a broken Blood Axe standing-stone ring. It should read as a once-upright Giant-scale slab that collapsed long ago, sank into cold ash, mud, dry grass, and disturbed sockets, and now interrupts the old ring continuity by showing time passing rather than active destruction.

The asset is abandoned Blood Axe camp memory and warning dressing only. It is not terrain integration, gameplay cover, traversal proof, interaction prompt, pickup behavior, collision correctness, route marker, objective marker, or first implementation target. Its visual role is to make the broken ring feel old, incomplete, and abandoned without becoming a mechanic.

Blood Axe visual language must stay separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture. The Blood Axe Tribe is a hostile Giant sub-faction and does not define all Giants. Neutral/civilized Giant culture remains associated with hidden cave towns, master stonework, terraces, bridges, waterworks, hearth light, blue-gray civic masonry, and restrained storm or rune accents; none of those are the default read for this package.
