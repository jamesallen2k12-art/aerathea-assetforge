## 10. LOD Plan

All future important versions require LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: Full cairn stacking, broad bevels, visible wedge gap, dull scrap cap or shield rim, major base ash/mud shape, optional large lashing, and readable red paint smear if used.
- LOD1: 60-70 percent of LOD0; simplify stone bevels, reduce underside cuts, merge small base chips, reduce metal edge bends, and flatten minor lashing detail.
- LOD2: 35-45 percent of LOD0; preserve cairn mass and scrap-cap silhouette while simplifying individual stones, removing secondary creases, and baking small overlap detail into textures.
- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0; preserve only the broad stone pile, wedged scrap silhouette, and base footprint; remove small bindings, back-side detail, chips, dents, and minor ash geometry.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny stone chips, ash flecks, soot speckles, scratches, dents, and paint chips.
2. Minor lashing fibers, small mud ridges, secondary stone cracks, and shallow metal pitting.
3. Small bevels on underside or back-facing stones.
4. Secondary bends in the scrap cap or shield rim.
5. Non-essential base ash shapes and back-side overlap cuts.

Never reduce the primary cairn mass, the wedged scrap-cap/shield-rim read, or the hostile Blood Axe red/black material language before removing micro-detail.
