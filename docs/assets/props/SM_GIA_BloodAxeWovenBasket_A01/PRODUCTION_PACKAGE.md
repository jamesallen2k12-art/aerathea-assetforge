# SM_GIA_BloodAxeWovenBasket_A01 Production Package

Docs-only production package for a single Blood Axe Giant woven utility basket. This package creates no DCC source, no Unreal Content, no startup placement, no final approval, and no implementation target.

## 1. Art Direction Summary

`SM_GIA_BloodAxeWovenBasket_A01` is a single Giant-scale woven utility basket for hostile Blood Axe camp dressing. It should read as rough, oversized, practical storage clutter made from thick reeds, split timber strips, rawhide bands, and a large reinforced rim. The basket is not refined craft, not a market display, and not a usable container.

Blood Axe remains a hostile Giant sub-faction only. This asset must stay visually separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture: no blue-gray cave-town masonry language, no warm hearth supply styling, no refined highland craft, no civic stoneworker motifs, no orderly market goods, and no restrained blue-rune identity.

This package explicitly defines static dressing only. It defines no loot, pickup, vendor, resource, inventory, storage UI, interaction, destructible behavior, nav behavior, physics, cloth, VFX/audio, DCC work, Unreal asset creation, startup placement, final approval, source concept movement, or Hermes work.

## 3. Silhouette Notes

Primary silhouette:

- One large, blunt, Giant-scale basket with a squat-to-tall utility profile.
- Thick oversized rim as the strongest top read.
- Slightly uneven round or oval footprint with a broad stable base.
- Simplified broad weave bands around the body, not dense fine wicker.
- Large vertical ribs or split timber uprights spaced widely enough to read from MMO camera distance.
- Rawhide bands or lashings cinching the rim, belly, or base in a few strong shapes.

Shape hierarchy:

- First read: oversized basket body and large reinforced rim.
- Second read: broad weave rhythm and thick vertical ribs.
- Third read: rawhide bands, large knots, rough repairs, and base reinforcement.
- Fourth read: soot, ash, mud, reed cuts, frayed fibers, scratches, and small splits through texture and normals.

Model real geometry for the main basket body, large rim, broad weave bands, vertical ribs, base ring, rawhide bands, and large knots. Fake reed fibers, thin splits, tiny fray, fine dirt speckles, scratch marks, small cuts, and minor weave texture with maps.

Avoid human-scale basket language, cozy market styling, tidy produce-basket cues, refined domestic storage, polished reed craft, vendor display layout, visible sellable goods, treasure-basket shape, UI-like tags, magic glow, neutral/civilized Giant motifs, many tiny weave strips, or fragile wicker detail that would not survive a Blood Axe camp read.

## 10. LOD Plan

All future approved mesh work should include LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: full Giant-scale basket body, large reinforced rim, broad weave bands, vertical ribs, base support, rawhide bands, large knot or repair tie, restrained oxide red accent if used, and grime through texture.
- LOD1: 55-70 percent of LOD0. Preserve body shape, rim thickness, broad weave rhythm, main ribs, base ring, rawhide bands, and largest knot. Reduce secondary bevels, interior strip thickness, minor asymmetry, and small lashing tails.
- LOD2: 25-40 percent of LOD0. Preserve basket silhouette, rim band, a simplified broad weave read, several major ribs, base mass, and one rawhide-band read. Move most reed detail and repair detail into texture.
- LOD3: 8-18 percent of LOD0. Preserve a simple oversized basket mass with a strong rim and dark weave texture. Rawhide bands and red accent may become texture-only if the hostile Blood Axe read remains clear.

LOD reduction order:

1. Reed fibers, tiny fray, fine splits, soot speckles, ash flecks, mud flecks, paint chips, thin scratches, and minor weave texture.
2. Small lashing tails, small repair wraps, secondary knot cuts, and minor rim chips.
3. Interior strip relief and hidden backside weave thickness.
4. Secondary vertical ribs and nonessential weave bands.
5. Minor base unevenness and small rim bevels.
6. Only after these reductions, simplify the main basket body, large rim, broad weave read, base mass, and rawhide-band identity.

Never destroy the Giant-scale basket mass, large rim, broad weave rhythm, or hostile Blood Axe material identity before reducing small detail.
