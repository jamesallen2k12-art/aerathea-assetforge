`SM_GIA_BloodAxeDryChannelBrokenSection_A01` is a dry, inactive broken middle section for Blood Axe dry-channel stone layouts. It should read as a fractured Giant-scale trough slab with a split channel, missing side mass, offset slab halves, ash-packed gaps, soot-darkened recesses, and broad static damage baked into the form. The break is old and inert: it is not a destructible state, liquid break, damage path, gameplay conduit, trigger, puzzle element, or VFX/audio source.

Blood Axe visual language must stay tied to a hostile Giant raider sub-faction and must remain separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture. Use crude abandoned-camp residue, rough highland stone, soot, cold ash, trampled mud, blackened iron, rope, rawhide, sparse horn or dull bone, and restrained oxide red warning accents. Do not use refined cave-town masonry, blue-gray civic stonework, terrace or waterwork motifs, warm hearth identity, restrained blue runes, peaceful highland wayfinding, or polished Giant stoneworker craft language.
