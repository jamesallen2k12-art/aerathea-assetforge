`SM_GIA_BloodAxeCavePaintedSlab_A01` defines a low static slab variant for Blood Axe cave-edge remnant dressing. The slab is rough highland stone with chipped dirty red paint and worn Blood Axe warning swipes, grounded by soot, ash, mud, and cave grit. Paint is visual history only: old hostile occupation residue, not an active sign system.

Blood Axe remains a hostile Giant sub-faction separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture. This slab may look crude, damaged, abandoned, and threatening, but it must not become the default Giant cultural stone language. Neutral/civilized Giant culture remains tied to skilled stonework, blue-gray civic masonry, cave-town terraces, waterworks, hearth warmth, monumental masonry, and restrained civic rune identity.

## LOD Plan

All future important static meshes require LOD0, LOD1, LOD2, and LOD3 before any shipping use.

- LOD0: full slab silhouette, visible thickness, major chipped edges, broad broken planes, large base contact, dirty red paint read through texture, soot/ash/mud grounding, and top/side readability.
- LOD1: 55-70 percent of LOD0; reduce secondary chips, small bevels, underside cuts, base undercuts, and non-silhouette fracture subdivisions while preserving the painted slab read.
- LOD2: 30-45 percent of LOD0; merge stone planes, flatten small chips, simplify broken edges, reduce base geometry, and keep the broad slab mass plus dirty red paint zone readable.
- LOD3: 12-25 percent of LOD0; preserve only the main low slab block, asymmetrical outline, grounded value read, and broad paint/value read.

Reduction order:

1. Stone pores, fine cracks, paint chips, soot speckles, ash flecks, mud droplets, lichen, mineral streaks, grit, and small scratches.
2. Tiny chipped cuts, minor bevels, shallow non-silhouette cracks, and little base nicks.
3. Back-facing fractures, underside cuts, buried contact undercuts, and hidden base detail.
4. Secondary rock-plane subdivisions and minor edge breaks.
5. Large bevel density and base-settle geometry.
6. Only after small detail is removed, simplify the primary slab silhouette and broad painted warning read.

Never reduce Giant-scale readability, the Blood Axe dirty red warning accent, or the static low slab silhouette before removing small detail.
