## LOD Plan

All future shipping-quality mesh work requires LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: full thick coil turns, blunt tied end or knot, broad wrap, optional red accent, and underside flattening.
- LOD1: 55-65 percent of LOD0; merge minor turn bevels, simplify knot undercuts, reduce wrap folds, and remove small underside cuts.
- LOD2: 25-40 percent of LOD0; preserve the coil footprint, inner opening, one tied-end silhouette, and red accent while flattening secondary turn detail.
- LOD3: 10-20 percent of LOD0; preserve a simple oval coil read with one end bump and one dark/red material accent if needed.

Reduction order:

1. Rope fibers, fine fray, soot speckles, mud flecks, small scratches, and minor cracks.
2. Small wrap wrinkles, tiny tie-end notches, and secondary bevels.
3. Minor turn asymmetry and hidden underside detail.
4. Knot undercuts, optional ring bevels, and non-silhouette interior detail.

Never reduce the main coil footprint, thick-turn read, or blunt tied-end silhouette before small detail is removed.
