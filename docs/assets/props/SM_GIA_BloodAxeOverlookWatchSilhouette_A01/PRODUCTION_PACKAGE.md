## LOD Plan

All future approved mesh work should include LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: 4k-9k tris target, 12k hard cap. Full silhouette with main post cluster, shallow edge mass, rail fragments, bold braces, warning pole, red cloth, large clamps, major lashings, cliff-contact read, and sparse non-graphic accents.
- LOD1: 60-70 percent of LOD0. Reduce minor post chips, small rail breaks, clamp bevels, cloth edge cuts, secondary lashing turns, small mud forms, and backside detail.
- LOD2: 35-45 percent of LOD0. Merge or simplify rail fragments, reduce brace subdivisions, flatten minor bands and wraps, simplify cloth shape, remove most small accents, and keep only large hardware blocks.
- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0. Preserve the recognizable overlook profile: tall posts, shallow upper edge, one rail line, diagonal brace read, warning pole or red cloth beat, and hostile Blood Axe silhouette.

LOD reduction order:

1. Tiny scratches, stitch rows, nail heads, rope fibers, soot speckles, ash flecks, pitting, small cracks, and cloth weave.
2. Small straps, minor knots, little chips, cloth holes, bark cuts, and fine plank splits.
3. Secondary horn, bone, shield, blade-offcut, and red-paint accents.
4. Backside details, hidden cliff-contact pieces, minor rail breaks, and non-visible brace backs.
5. Clamp bevels, post chip complexity, cloth fold geometry, and metal plate undercuts.
6. Only after secondary detail is removed, simplify the primary post cluster, upper silhouette edge, warning pole, and red warning read.

Never destroy the primary skyline silhouette, Giant-scale relationship, Blood Axe warning read, or non-walkable shallow profile before removing small detail.
