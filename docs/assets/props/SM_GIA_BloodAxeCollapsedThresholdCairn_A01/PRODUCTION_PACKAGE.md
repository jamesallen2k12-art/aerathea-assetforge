- LOD1: 60-70 percent of LOD0; reduce bevel density, small chip geometry, underside cuts, minor stone notches, cloth edge cuts, and base undercuts.

- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0; preserve only the low collapsed cairn mass, broad threshold footprint, one or two major shifted-stone reads, and one subdued red accent if present.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny cracks, pitting, soot speckles, ash flecks, lichen, cloth weave, fray, paint chips, and scratches.

3. Small foot stones, non-silhouette debris, minor horn or iron scraps, and duplicate base details.
4. Backside detail, secondary decorative cuts, and extra base undulation.
5. Stone bevel density, cloth fold geometry, and large ash/mud edge subdivisions.
6. Only after smaller details are removed, simplify the primary collapsed-stone silhouette.

Never reduce the Giant-scale read, low abandoned threshold footprint, Blood Axe red accent restraint, or main collapsed cairn silhouette before removing smaller detail.
