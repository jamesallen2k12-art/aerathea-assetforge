`SM_GIA_BloodAxeOpenHideRoll_A01` defines a partly unrolled hide with one broad flap and a visible rough underside for hostile Blood Axe camp-life read. It should feel like an oversized, battered Giant-owned hide spread left near a shelter edge: one compressed roll lip, one wide static flap, coarse underside texture, thick rawhide tie remnants, soot-dark grime, and restrained oxide-red Blood Axe identifiers.

## LOD Plan

All important variants require LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: full roll lip, broad flap, visible underside curl, thick edges, large folds, strap bands, red accent, and ground-contact shape.
- LOD1: 60-70 percent of LOD0; reduce minor fold subdivisions, strap bevels, underside edge cuts, small tie ends, and secondary silhouette waviness.
- LOD2: 35-45 percent of LOD0; preserve roll mass, broad flap footprint, underside color block, red accent, and Giant-scale shape while flattening small folds and hidden underside detail.
- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0; preserve the low camp-clutter silhouette, roll-to-flap read, broad Blood Axe material block, and red/dark accent read. Remove most small lashing and edge detail.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny hide pores, underside nap, stitch impressions, scratches, soot speckles, and mud flecks.
2. Small fray cuts, fine edge waviness, minor strap notches, and small tie ends.
3. Secondary fold ridges, underside wrinkles, and interior contact creases.
4. Optional tiny hardware, back-facing strap detail, and hidden underside geometry.
5. Roll lip bevel subdivisions and non-essential ground-contact chips.

Never reduce the main roll silhouette, broad open flap footprint, visible underside read, Giant-scale proportions, or Blood Axe red accent before removing small detail.
