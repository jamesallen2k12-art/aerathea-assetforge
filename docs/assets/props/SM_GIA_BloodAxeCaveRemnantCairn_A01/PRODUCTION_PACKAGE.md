## LOD Plan

All future static mesh implementation requires LOD0, LOD1, LOD2, and LOD3 before shipping use.

- LOD0: full low cairn silhouette, all major stones, broad ash/mud base, optional low cloth, and broad rope/rawhide.
- LOD1: reduce stone edge loops, remove small chips, simplify cloth edge and rope lashings.
- LOD2: preserve main cairn mass, largest stone planes, broad ground contact, and cloth color block if present.
- LOD3: simple low silhouette preserving broad cave-remnant read only.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny cracks, soot speckles, lichen, cloth fibers, rope fibers, and small chips.
2. Secondary cloth fray, minor stone edge chips, and small ground grit.
3. Optional small debris, horn/bone/iron accents, and secondary rope lashings.
4. Major stone silhouettes, low cairn height, and cave-edge ground contact only after all small detail is removed.

Never preserve micro-detail at the expense of the low old cave-remnant silhouette.
