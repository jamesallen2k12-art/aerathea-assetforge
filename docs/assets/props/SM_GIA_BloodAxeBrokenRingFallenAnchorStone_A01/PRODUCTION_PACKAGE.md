It should read as a former major ring stone that has collapsed long ago, settled low, and become partly sunk into cold ash and trampled mud. The slab is static collapse history within a broken standing-stone ring, not a current event, moving hazard, interactive object, or gameplay device.

The asset is abandoned Blood Axe camp memory and warning dressing only. It may imply that a hostile Giant raider camp once marked the location and then abandoned, stripped, or ruined it. 4. Secondary marker tokens, small iron scraps, minor bone or horn accents, and small cloth knots.
5. Stone bevel density, secondary fracture cuts, cloth fold geometry, and non-silhouette ground detail.
