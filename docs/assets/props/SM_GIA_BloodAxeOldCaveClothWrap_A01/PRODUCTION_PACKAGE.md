All future important static mesh implementation requires LOD0, LOD1, LOD2, and LOD3 before shipping use.

- LOD0: full cloth wrap silhouette, broad fixed fold channels, thickened edges, tied or trapped contact, optional support stone, soot/mud grounding, and optional static rope/rawhide accent.
- LOD1: 55-70 percent of LOD0; reduce secondary fold cuts, minor edge waviness, small stone bevels, backside overlap detail, and optional accent bevels while preserving the tied/trapped red cloth read.

4. Optional blackened iron pin, old horn chip, dull bone token, small wedge stone, or secondary paint smear.
5. Cloth thickness subdivisions, major fold subdivisions, tie bevel density, and stone/slab bevel density.
