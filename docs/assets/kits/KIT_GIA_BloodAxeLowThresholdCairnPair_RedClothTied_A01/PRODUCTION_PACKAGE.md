`KIT_GIA_BloodAxeLowThresholdCairnPair_RedClothTied_A01` defines a paired low-threshold cairn kit where one or both squat Giant-built stone clusters are tied with broad static oxide red cloth strips using rope, rawhide, hide, or sinew. The pair should read as a crude Blood Axe territorial threshold beat: low, heavy, old, soot-stained, and hostile without becoming a gate, sign, UI marker, quest pointer, objective cue, or route instruction.

The cloth is a restrained faction accent, not a banner system. It should be broad enough for MMO camera readability, tied tightly enough to feel fixed, and aged through soot, ash, mud, and faded dye. Blood Axe identity must remain a hostile Giant sub-faction language and must not overwrite neutral/civilized Giant culture, which remains tied to master stonework, hidden highland cave towns, blue-gray civic masonry, terraces, waterworks, warm hearth identity, and restrained civic runes.

- Primary read: two squat low cairns with one or both cairns tied by broad oxide red cloth strips.
- The pair should stay low and threshold-like, built from a few large stones rather than dense pebble piles.
- One cairn may carry the stronger cloth tie while the other uses a smaller tied strip, or both may carry matching restrained cloth wraps.
