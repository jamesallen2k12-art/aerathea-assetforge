`KIT_GIA_BloodAxeHideRollBundle_A01` defines a reusable Blood Axe storage-dressing kit made from low, heavy, roll-like covered bundles. The read should be practical and hostile: rough hide wraps pulled around bulky hidden contents, thick rope or rawhide bands cinched across the mass, compressed dirt-heavy bases, mud at ground contact, and small restrained oxide red ownership accents.

This package is for static storage dressing at shelter edges, lean-to sides, camp wall bases, and path-adjacent Blood Axe clutter. It must not read as a usable bedroll, resting station, inventory bundle, loot cache, pickup object, vendor stock, resource node, cloth simulation test, destructible prop, or interactive camp object.

Blood Axe remains a hostile Giant sub-faction. This kit must stay separate from neutral/civilized Giant culture: no clean cave-town storage, no blue-gray civic masonry, no warm hearth bedding, no refined highland market bundle, no restrained blue rune identity, and no peaceful Giant settlement motifs.

## 3. Silhouette Notes

Primary read:

- One composed low bundle kit or a small set of two to four roll-like storage masses.
- Broad, flattened ground-contact forms with more weight than a loose blanket or bedding roll.
- Hide wraps pulled over bulky hidden shapes, with large overlap lips and simple end folds.
- Thick rope, sinew, or rawhide bands crossing the bundle in one or two readable directions.
- Large knots or cinch points scaled for Giant hands, kept sparse and chunky.
- Mud-dark lower contact, compressed underside, and soot-stained top planes.
- Optional small oxide red cloth tag, wrap, or rubbed mark as Blood Axe identity only.

Shape hierarchy:

- First read: heavy Giant-scale covered storage mass.
- Second read: roll-like hide wrap with broad end folds.
- Third read: thick rope or rawhide bands holding the bundle shut.
- Fourth read: compressed ground contact and mud-dark base.
- Fifth read: patch seams, hide grain, rope fibers, soot, ash, fray, and small scratches handled through texture and normal detail.

Model real geometry for the main roll-like covered masses, large hide overlap lips, end folds, thick rope bands, big knots, and strongest ground-contact deformation. Avoid bed pillows, sleeping blankets, neatly tied luggage, merchant display bundles, treasure-bag silhouettes, readable labels, UI tags, glow accents, refined neutral Giant craft marks, cozy domestic bedding, and any shape that suggests pickup, inventory, bed use, or searchable loot.

## 10. LOD Plan

All future approved mesh work should include LOD0-LOD3.

- LOD0: full low storage-bundle composition with varied roll-like masses, broad hide wraps, large overlap lips, blunt end folds, thick rope/rawhide bands, large knots, compressed bases, mud contact, and sparse Blood Axe red accents.
- LOD1: preserve the full footprint, mass count, thick bands, largest knots, and strongest hide folds while reducing secondary fold cuts, underside detail, small tabs, and minor band bevels.
- LOD2: preserve the low Giant-scale storage silhouette, main band reads, broad end folds, and red accent block while flattening smaller hide overlaps, simplifying knots, and removing most hidden back-side detail.
- LOD3: retain the broad grounded bundle mass, darkest base, two or three band reads, and hostile Blood Axe material color blocks. Remove small tabs, secondary knots, minor fold lips, and small metal accents.

LOD reduction order:

1. Tiny stitches, hide pores, rope fibers, cloth weave, soot speckles, ash flecks, mud grains, paint chips, and fine scratches.
2. Small fray fibers, minor tab tears, tiny band notches, small red marks, and secondary surface cuts.
3. Underside folds, hidden back-facing overlaps, small tie tails, and secondary compression dents.
4. Small metal ring bevels, knot undercuts, and non-structural band thickness.
5. Secondary hide lip cuts and minor mass asymmetry.

Never reduce the primary low ground-contact mass, roll-like hide-wrap read, thick rope band read, Giant-scale footprint, or Blood Axe hostile material identity before removing small detail.
