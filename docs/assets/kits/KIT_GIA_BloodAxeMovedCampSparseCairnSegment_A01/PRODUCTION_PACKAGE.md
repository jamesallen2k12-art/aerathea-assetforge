## LOD Plan

All future shipping use requires LOD0, LOD1, LOD2, and LOD3 after separate approval.

- LOD0: full 2-4 remnant composition, major stones, broad ash/mud base, small fixed cloth remnants, primary lashings, and rare non-graphic accents.
- LOD1: 60-70 percent of LOD0; reduce bevel density, minor stone cuts, underside contact detail, cloth edge cuts, small lashing loops, and optional scrap.
- LOD2: 35-45 percent of LOD0; merge small support stones, flatten ash/mud forms, simplify cloth to one or two broad shapes, and preserve only the main broken spacing.
- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0; preserve the largest low cairn masses, one missing beat, dark ground value, and one restrained oxide red cloth read.

Reduction order:

1. Tiny chips, hairline cracks, ash flecks, soot speckles, cloth weave, fray cuts, scratches, lichen, and paint chips.
2. Small rope fibers, secondary lashings, minor knots, hidden underside detail, and small non-silhouette stones.
3. Small cloth holes, small horn or bone chips, minor iron scraps, and low debris.
4. Backside overlaps, base undercuts, duplicate stone contacts, and secondary decorative cuts.
5. Small bevels on supporting stones.
6. Only after small detail is removed, simplify the main cairn remnant silhouettes and missing-beat rhythm.

Never reduce the Giant-scale remnant read, the short broken span identity, the abandoned moved-camp history, or the Blood Axe red/black material identity before removing small detail.
