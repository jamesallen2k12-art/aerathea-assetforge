- LOD3: 15-25 percent of LOD0; preserve two dominant stone heights, the broken arc read, the missing-ring negative space, and one restrained Blood Axe red accent only.

1. Tiny cracks, small chips, cloth weave, rope fibers, soot speckles, ash flecks, lichen specks, paint chips, and minor scratches.
2. Small lashings, tiny horn chips, small stone wedges, secondary cloth tears, and minor metal nicks.
3. Back-facing chock stones, underside slab bevels, hidden base cuts, and non-visible ash/mud undercuts.
4. Secondary marker tokens, small iron scraps, minor bone or horn accents, and small cloth knots.
5. Stone bevel density, secondary fracture cuts, cloth fold geometry, and non-silhouette ground detail.
6. Only after small detail is gone, simplify the two dominant stones, visible absence, major ground contact, and Blood Axe accent reads.

Never reduce the Giant-scale relationship, two-dominant-stone silhouette, visible missing-ring absence, abandoned-camp memory read, or Blood Axe/civilized Giant separation before removing small detail.
