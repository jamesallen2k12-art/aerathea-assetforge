## Notes

This sheet is a Blood Axe armory catalog, not a neutral Giant culture kit. It shows brutal raider weapons, bowyer equipment, reforged scrap-metal language, trophy armor, red cloth, blackened iron, skull trophies, forge-adjacent camp reference, and process notes for stolen metal being broken down and reforged into Blood Axe war gear.

All child assets must use the validated `SK_GIA_Base_A01` scale lock before DCC work: female Giant baseline 442 cm / 14'6", male Giant baseline 470 cm / 15'5", with approved race ranges of females 14-15 ft and males 14'10"-16'0". Weapons, bows, armor straps, quivers, and trophy plates must be fitted to Giant hand, back, belt, head, torso, and foot sockets. Normal humanoid compatibility is not required unless a future encounter or loot-display task asks for scaled-down trophy props.
