# P01E Tesseract Projection Notes

- A tesseract is a 4D hypercube.
- The Blender scene represents it as a 3D projection with all 16 vertices and 32 edges.
- Gray edges are ordinary X/Y/Z cube edges.
- Blue edges are W-dimension connector edges between the paired cubes.
- Gold vertices belong to the outer projected cube; blue vertices belong to the inner projected cube.
- This is a training proof, not a game-ready asset candidate.
