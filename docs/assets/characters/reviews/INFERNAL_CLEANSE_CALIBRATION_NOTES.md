## Decision

- A01 and A02 are rejected for production because they removed too much Infernal menace, glow, skull/bone threat language, and power.
- A04 is not the default because it can reduce eye glow, magic glow, and useful character detail.
- A03 is the approved default. Use A04 only when A03 remains too noisy, and only if villain threat, skull/bone iconography, fire, lightning-like energy, glowing eyes, and major silhouette detail are preserved.
- The base 30 percent brightness outputs are work-in-progress utility files, not final approval images.

## Cleanup Policy

Keep the local calibration images only while actively comparing cleanup strength. They can be archived or deleted later after the next review cycle confirms no further A03/A04 comparison is needed. Do not promote these images into production docs unless the user explicitly approves them.
