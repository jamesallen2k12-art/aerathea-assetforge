# Infernal A03 Cleanse Review Index

## Approved Direction

The approved default for Infernal and Lesser Infernal cleanup is A03-style:

- Start from a roughly 30 percent brighter pass when the source is too dark.
- Preserve skulls, bones, flame, lightning-like abyssal energy, glowing eyes, anger, menace, and major silhouette detail.
- Remove tiny repeated rivets, artifact speckles, malformed micro-spikes, broken micro-chains, noisy gold flecks, and texture garbage.
- Keep ground and environment texture noise as a lower priority than character readability; ground can be changed later.

Use A04-style cleanup only when the image remains too noisy after A03. A04 must not reduce eye glow, magic glow, villain threat, or major skull/bone iconography.

## Production Use

- Treat adult standing images as body, material, silhouette, and class anchors.
- Treat airborne and duel images as action, VFX, encounter, and mood references.
- Do not model tiny rivets, repeated micro-spikes, speckle artifacts, or noisy ground cracks.
- Model horns, claws, wing bones, tail mass, major armor plates, major skull ornaments, and major props as real geometry.
- Push fine cracks, membrane veins, small scratches, and minor ornament detail into texture, normal, AO, ORM, and emissive maps.
