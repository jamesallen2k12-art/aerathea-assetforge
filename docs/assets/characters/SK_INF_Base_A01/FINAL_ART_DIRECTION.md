# SK_INF_Base_A01 Final Art Direction Sheet

## Art Direction Summary

`SK_INF_Base_A01` is the production body/style sheet for adult Infernals. The first-pass compact and tall blockouts are approved for scale and silhouette, so this sheet locks the adult design language for sculpt, retopo, UV, texture, rig, animation, and future class variants.

Infernals should read as a mortal-descended demonic race that willingly embraced Balgoroth's gifts. They are not generic demons and not true Abyss creatures. Their base identity is a society of predatory, ambitious, cult-bound people whose bodies have become weapons: horns, large leathery wings, long thick tails, black claws, regenerative scar language, skull/bone villain iconography where appropriate, and readable eye/brand glow.

## Gameplay Purpose

Adult Infernals support playable race exploration, hostile cult factions, elite NPCs, hunters, rogues, warriors, mages, ritual leaders, and encounter bosses. Their visual package must work without mortal weapon dependency. Even when a class later uses a tool, relic, or focus, the core combat read remains claws, tail, wings, teeth, body power, magic, and demonic gifts.

## Locked Adult Body Bands

The adult canon range remains 5'0"-9'0" / 152-274 cm. Production should use body-type bands instead of a strict male/female height split until character creation rules demand otherwise.

| Body band | Height range | Primary use | Silhouette rule |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Compact | 5'0"-5'8" / 152-173 cm | rogues, scouts, hunters, low-profile cultists | leaner frame, swept horns, folded wings kept tight, fast tail balance |
| Standard | 5'8"-6'8" / 173-203 cm | common adults, warriors, mages, hunters | athletic frame, readable horn mass, full folded wings, thick tail |
| Greater | 6'8"-8'0" / 203-244 cm | elites, commanders, heavy warriors, senior mages | broader chest, larger horns, heavier wing roots, stronger tail base |
| Exalted | 8'0"-9'0" / 244-274 cm | champions, cult lords, rare ancient-blood adults | imposing wing mantle, heavy horns, ritual marks, controlled posture |

Sex-specific variants can be layered later through face, torso, hip, voice, hair/no-hair, horn style, stance, and outfit language. Do not let sex variants break the horn/wing/tail/claw requirements or move outside the adult height band.

## Silhouette Notes

- The readable long-distance silhouette is horn crown, folded wing mass, thick tail, black claws, upright predatory humanoid.
- Horns are real geometry. Use four approved families: swept-back blade horns, crown horns, forward-hook horns, and broken/asymmetric veteran horns.
- Wings must read clearly when folded. Default gameplay fold is tight enough for doorways and navigation; spread wings are reserved for flare, leap, glide, intimidation, cast, and boss states.
- Tail is thick at the root, muscular through the midsection, and expressive at the tip. It must never look like a thin decorative cord.
- Hands and feet carry black claws. The hands need a claw-strike pose language even at neutral idle.
- Face remains human-derived but transformed: strong brow, horn roots, predatory eyes, controlled rage, and visible intelligence.
- Regeneration reads as healed seam scars, subtle ember-vein patterning, and new skin contrast, not open gore.

## Concept Image Prompt

Create an original stylized fantasy MMORPG adult race production sheet of Infernals for the world of Aerathea. The design should emphasize a mortal-descended demonic race blessed by Balgoroth, adult body bands from compact 5'0" rogues to exalted 9'0" champions, reddish skin, black claws, required horns, large folded leathery wings, long thick tails, regenerative healed-scar language, invisible-sight eye glow, natural-weapon combat, predatory upright posture, cult hierarchy, major skull/bone villain iconography where appropriate, flame or lightning-like abyssal power effects for caster variants, and readable abyssal brand marks. Use hand-painted texture detail, readable shapes, baked-AO-style depth, normal-map-style surface detail, focused emissive accents, and MMO-friendly production design. Present it as a clean character production sheet with compact, standard, greater, and exalted body bands, front/side/back views, folded and spread wing callouts, horn families, claw detail, tail motion callouts, material swatches, and a 180 cm humanoid scale marker. Clean the design for production readability: full body visible, feet and wings uncropped, readable midtone lighting, clear separation between red skin, black armor, dark wings, and background. If the source is too dark, start from a roughly 30 percent brighter pass. Use A03-style cleanup by default: reduce tiny repeated rivets, random speckle artifacts, malformed micro-spikes, broken micro-chains, torn-strip noise, dense spell clutter that hides anatomy, and photoreal surface garbage. Preserve skulls, bones, flame, lightning, glowing eyes, anger, and villain threat. Avoid copied franchise designs, gore, excessive particles, readable text, watermarks, and weapon-focused designs.

## Modeling Notes

- Model real geometry for body planes, horns, claws, wings, tail, major scars, major ritual ornaments, and large brands if raised.
- Use texture and normal maps for skin grain, fine scars, membrane veins, horn striation, tiny brands, cloth weave, leather scratches, and bead surface wear.
- Retopo must support facial expression, claw attacks, tail bends, wing fold/spread, leap, cast, and rage idle.
- Wing bones need folded default, half flare, full spread, damaged fold, and casting mantle poses.
- Tail rig needs root, lower mid, upper mid, and tip controls. Tail should support balance, sweep attack, hit reaction, and idle expression.
- Claw hands should be posed and weighted for open palm, hook grip, slash, rake, and spell-channel gestures.

## LOD Plan

- LOD0: full body, face, horns, claws, wings, tail, major ritual ornaments, major scars, socket landmarks.
- LOD1: 55-60 percent; reduce minor scars, small straps, wing edge cuts, claw bevels, secondary ornament bevels.
- LOD2: 25-35 percent; simplify fingers, membrane folds, horn bevels, tail taper, small brands.
- LOD3: 10-15 percent; preserve body height, horns, folded wings, thick tail, black claws, red/black color read, and glow blocks.

## Collision Notes

- Movement capsule per body band, with folded wings ignored by default navigation.
- Tail uses simplified physics bodies only for combat, hit reaction, and authored secondary motion.
- Wings use simplified non-blocking physics for animation and hit reaction unless a specific ability requires collision.
- Suggested bodies: pelvis, spine, chest, head, upper/lower arms, hands, thighs, calves, feet, tail root/mid/tip, wing upper/lower simplified bodies.

## Animation Notes

Required base set:

- Idle calm, idle restrained rage, walk, run, turn, jump, land, hit react, death, interact.
- Claw combo, rake, pounce, tail sweep, wing buffet, leap, execute pose.
- See-invisible focus, regeneration flare, brand activation, mage channel, wing flare, folded-to-spread transition.

Class overlays:

- Warrior: heavier stance, wing-guard posture, broad claw arcs, tail counterweight.
- Rogue: compact fold, low stalking posture, fast rake, short leap, shadowed brand glow.
- Hunter: tracking posture, elevated sight gesture, pounce/leap, wing-assisted reposition.
- Mage: ritual hand shapes, brand-channel poses, wing mantle, eye/forehead glow timing.
